About Us


Helping People Find Volunteering Positions Since 2018

NP Volunteer was founded by four freshmen. There are two big problems for volunteering in New Providence: organizations can't find volunteers and volunteers cannot connect with organizations to get volunteering positions. So, we aim to solve the problem by making a website that lists all the orginizations that are looking for volunteers. It can also find users the perfect volunteering positions based on their needs and the organizations needs.
